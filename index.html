About Re-Vision

Re-Vision is a platform that utilizes computer vision to analyze 3D spatial twins of properties. This allows instant recognition of defects and important housing properties. Insights can be generated and combined with various aiding datasets to generate increasingly accurate predictions about the status of properties at scale.
